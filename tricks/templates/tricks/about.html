About Django Tricks

What is this

Django Tricks was created to help Django community find and share interesting ways of using Django framework

The idea is simple: When you work with Django long enough you find some cool ways of using it, and to other people they might seem as "tricks" so we thought to create a centralized place for all Django users to share those findings.

Who?

Django Tricks website was created by Prosper Otemuyiwa in May 2015
